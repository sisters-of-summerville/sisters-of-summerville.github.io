Sisters of Summerville
Daily Comic Strip · Summerville, SC

Artist David Fliesen has been partnering with multiple AI to make these daily comics about his real life dog and cat who live in Summerville, SC. He uses this to tell their story while also expressing his creative side and forcing himself to work with multiple Artificial Intelligence (AI) applications every day.

🎨 See more of my work in my portfolio →

Honey Bear (a sassy Yorkie) and Bootsie Belle (a scheming tuxedo cat) get into fresh trouble powered by AI image generation and an artist's whole lot of love for pet chaos. This "Sisters of Summerville" blends a passion for AI, multimedia storytelling, and the very real personalities of two furry sisters.
